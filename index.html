The Artful Rachel

Welcome! The Artful Rachel is a project showcasing some artwork from Artful Rachel, a painter from Ireland. Rachel specialises in nature, landscapes and anything organic! Feel free to stay a while and browse the gallery, or order your very own custom artwork!

Art Styles and Mediums

Rachel specialises in nature paintings and anything organic, she has a special interest in Gouache and also uses Oils, Acrylic and Watercolour. If you want your own artwork you can choose which medium you prefer in the drop down menu on the commission form. There are also more examples of each in the Art section. Happy browsing!
